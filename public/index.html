front-end london
Next event 17th January, 2013 7pm - late • Islington

Front-end London is a free event hosted by Made by Many that's dedicated to all aspects of front-end development.

We aim to cover a wide variety of topics such as CSS, SASS / LESS, RWD and Javascript, with thought-provoking talks from a variety of speakers.

When is FEL?

Front-end London is a monthly event, starting on 17th January, 2013. 7pm - late.

What to expect

Great, focused talks by some of the best front-end developers in London. Topped off with beer and pizza and an obligatory trip to the local pub to boot.

We'll also be having a Q&A session with the speakers, on a particular topic decided every month and we might even have some other little special talks / interviews to make it an even better event.
