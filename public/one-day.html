Call for speakers

We're inviting the front-end community to submit proposals for the Front-end London day event. The event will take place around the end of August / beginning of September and as soon as we've finalised a date we'll let you know.

We're interested in hearing proposals from anybody, even if you've never spoken before. Primarily we're looking for topics that will excite us and that we'd love to hear a talk about (we're developers too).

Topics

The topics we're interested in hearing about are vast (see our previous speakers / talks over at Lanyard) they include, but are not limited to:

  • Javascript
  • CSS / Sass / LESS
  • Performance
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Tools / Best practises / Process

We aim to be on the cutting edge of front-end and have had some fantastic and thought provoking speakers previously and we'd love to carry on that trend with this event. We're aiming to bring the Front-end London experience to a larger audience in a unique location.

Details

  • Talks are usually 30 minutes long, with time allowed between for Q&A.
  • We're aiming for 6 speakers on the day, if we can fit in more we will.
  • The event will be held in London (Exact location TBA).
  • We may be able to cover your travel expenses, please get in touch.
